About Me

I currently live on the road, traveling around the US in my 2004 Freightliner Sprinter, Susanne, and work as a Web Developer at Firebelly, a design studio based in Chicago, Illinois.

I'm passionate about people, equality and happiness. I want to work on projects that have a positive impact on the world beyond just making someone money.

In my free time I enjoy rock climbing, skateboarding, endurance sports, and playing music. I love to travel and go on outdoor adventures, which I often photograph and (sometimes) post on Flickr.
